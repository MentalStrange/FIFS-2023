    Invitation

    During October 31-November 2, 2023, Animal and Fish Production Department, Faculty of Agriculture, Alexandria University in cooperation with the Egyptian Society of Animal Production will host the "6th International Conference on Future of Livestock and Food Security”. The conference emphasizes the global challenges and opportunities to animal research. We would like to invite colleagues to participate in this conference.

    About Us

    Future of Livestock’s and Food Security

    Objectives

    • The conference aims to encourage participation of scientists and stakeholders involved in livestock production industry to offer a great platform for learning, exchange opinions, cooperation and interaction academics. Also, provide an international idea for scientists and animal production professionals to Exchange Ideas, Knowledge and Networking.
    • Encouraging participation and interaction between scientists, researchers and farm owners in developing and finding a solution to the problems of animal production systems.
    • Developing livestock, fish and poultry for self-sufficiency and achieving food security from animal protein.

    Topics

    • Alternative feed resources for farm animals in marginal land.
    • Salt-tolerant forages in farm animals feeding.
    • Applicable strategies in utilization of Agri-industrial wastes.
    • Precise and smart livestock farming.
    • Traditional and molecular approaches of genetics improvement of farm animals.
    • Modern technology applications in the animal production industry.
    • Bio & Nano-technology approaches in livestock production.
    • Animal welfare, products and food security.
    • Fish farming and sustainable aquaculture.
    • Marketing of livestock products.
