    About Us

    Filling Gaps for Livestock Sustainability

    Objectives

    The international forum aims to discuss these challenges and opportunities, and to develop a roadmap to ﬁlling gaps to achieve food security, protect the environment, and sustain livestock.
    The forum provides opportunities for participating scientists, researchers, and producers to meet, discuss, and exchange experiences, ideas, and opinions. This gathering of prominent participants will help to better understand the problems and challenges facing animal production, as well as to ﬁnd practical solutions to these problems.

    Topics

    • Future of livestock in marginal and arid lands.
    • Vision of advance of Egyptian buffalo.
    • Biotechnology and genetic diversity.
    • Smart livestock production systems and bioinformatics.
    • Feeding systems and farm animal welfare.
    • Functional Genomics.
    • Climate change and climate adaptation of livestock.
    • Reproduction, immunity, and preventive care.
    • Poultry industry and its challenges.
    • Social impact of livestock.
