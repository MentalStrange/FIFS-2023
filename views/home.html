    Invitation

    During October 31-November 3, 2023, the Animal and Fish Production Department, Faculty of Agriculture, Alexandria University, and The Egyptian Society of Animal Production, Egypt will hold the joint Conference on “Future of Livestock and Food Security". The conference emphasizes the global challenges and opportunities for animal research. We would like to invite colleagues to participate in this conference.

    About Us

    Future of Livestock’s and Food Security

    Objectives

    The conference provides opportunities to participated scientists, researchers, stakeholders, and producers to meet, discuss, and exchange knowledge, ideas, and opinions.
    Such a gathering of prominent participants should help better understanding of problems and challenges that face animal production as well as find practical solutions to such problems.
    The conference also creates the chance for participants from different institutions to cooperate and benefit from each other.
    We are doing our best to make a conference good contribution to the animal production industry as the previous conferences if not better.

    Topics

    • Animal welfare, health, products and food security.
    • Alternative feed resources for farm animals in marginal land.
    • Salt-tolerant forages in farm animals feeding. wastes.
    • Applicable strategies in utilization of Agri-industrial wastes.
    • Precise and smart livestock farming.
    • Climate change and livestock sustainability.
    • Animal reproduction strategies for sustainable livestock production.
    • Traditional and molecular approaches of genetics improvement of farm animals.
    • Modern technology in the animal production industry.
    • Bio & Nano-technology approaches in livestock production.
    • Fish farming and sustainable aquaculture.
    • Marketing of livestock products.
